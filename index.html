Our Story

We founded Home Away From Home because of
our co-founder Kate Blumenthal. Kate and
Joe McKenzie were inspired from Kate's
personal journey of battling an illness
in the hospital. After enduring 70 days
hospitalized in 2023, grappling with an NJ
tube and multiple life-threatening bacterial
infections, Kate needed to give back once
her healing process began. Joe and Kate were
motivated to make a warmer, more comfortable
environment. This experience fueled our
mission to create spaces within healthcare
facilities that feel like home, providing
pure joy and support to patients during
their toughest times.

What We Do

We believe that home is a feeling not a
place, and our mission at Home Away
From Home is to ensure that nobody
gets homesick while staying at the
hospital. Maintaining mental health
is especially vital while in the
hospital, so we are taking steps to
make sure no child is negatively
impacted from the gloominess of a
hospital room by making their hospital
room looks just like their real room!

How To Help

By supporting Home Away From Home, your
donation will help more kids feel more at
home! It will help us to create and gather
more decorations, which will ultimately
allow us to decorate way more rooms with
more awesome decorations! Your support will
also help to bolster outreach beyond just the
decorating such as conducting drives and
spreading awareness about mental health
concerns for pediatric patients in the hospital!
