Our Story

We founded Home Away From Home because of our co-founder Kate Blumenthal. Kate and Joe McKenzie were inspired from Kate's personal journey of battling an illness in the hospital. After enduring 70 days hospitalized in 2023, grappling with an NJ tube and multiplelife-threatening bacterial infections, Kate needed to give back once her healing process began. Joe and Kate were motivated to make a warmer, more comfortable environment. This experience fueled our mission to create spaces within healthcare facilities that feel like home, providing pure joy and support to patients during their toughest times.

What We Do

We believe that home is a feeling not a place, and our mission at Home Away From Home is to ensure that nobody gets homesick while staying at the hospital. Maintaining mental health is especially vital while in the hospital, so we are taking steps to make sure no child is negatively impacted from the gloominess of a hospital room by making their hospital room looks just like their real room!

How To Help
