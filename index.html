Our Story

We founded Home Away From Home because of
our co-founder Kate Blumenthal. Kate and
Joe McKenzie were inspired from Kate's
personal journey of battling an illness
in the hospital. After enduring 70 days
hospitalized in 2023, grappling with an NJ
tube and multiple life-threatening bacterial
infections, Kate needed to give back once
her healing process began. Joe and Kate were
motivated to make a warmer, more comfortable
environment. This experience fueled our
mission to create spaces within healthcare
facilities that feel like home, providing
pure joy and support to patients during
their toughest times.
